# Burnout Detection Prompt

## Purpose

This prompt is designed to identify signs of mental exhaustion, productivity fatigue, emotional overload, and unhealthy work patterns from user journal entries.

The goal is to help NeuroLens AI recognize burnout indicators early and generate meaningful recovery-oriented recommendations.

---

# Burnout Detection Prompt

```text id="jlwm8y"
You are an AI cognitive wellness and productivity intelligence system.

Analyze the user's journal carefully for signs of:
- mental exhaustion
- emotional fatigue
- productivity burnout
- cognitive overload
- unhealthy work habits
- stress accumulation
- lack of recovery balance

Evaluate:
1. Burnout severity
2. Emotional exhaustion
3. Productivity sustainability
4. Stress patterns
5. Recovery imbalance
6. Long-term mental strain

Generate:
- a concise burnout analysis
- emotional interpretation
- behavioral observations
- recovery-focused recommendations

Maintain:
- empathetic tone
- analytical reasoning
- emotionally intelligent responses

Avoid:
- shallow motivational advice
- robotic responses
- exaggerated emotional assumptions
```

---

# Burnout Detection Strategy

The AI focuses on identifying patterns such as:

* continuous overworking
* reduced emotional satisfaction
* mental fatigue despite productivity
* stress accumulation
* sleep imbalance
* focus degradation
* emotional numbness

The system attempts to recognize burnout before it becomes severe.

---

# Example Burnout Input

```text id="jlwm4z"
I have been studying continuously for the last two weeks with very little sleep. Even after completing tasks I still feel mentally exhausted and unmotivated.
```

---

# Expected AI Interpretation

The AI should ideally identify:

* cognitive exhaustion
* unhealthy productivity cycle
* emotional fatigue
* recovery imbalance
* potential burnout risk

and generate balanced recommendations rather than generic encouragement.

---

# Contextual Memory Integration

Historical context is injected into the prompt to improve continuity.

Example:

```text id="jlwm6e"
Previous Observations:
- User reported reduced sleep for multiple consecutive entries
- Stress indicators have increased gradually
- Productivity pressure has remained consistently high
```

This helps the AI recognize long-term burnout progression.

---

# Structured Output Goals

The burnout analysis output is expected to generate:

* burnout score
* stress severity
* emotional exhaustion indicators
* productivity sustainability insights
* recovery recommendations

These metrics are later used in the analytics dashboard.

---

# Prompt Refinement Process

Several iterations were tested to improve:

* emotional sensitivity
* realism
* contextual continuity
* burnout reasoning quality
* recommendation usefulness

The prompt was refined to avoid overdramatic or generic AI responses.
